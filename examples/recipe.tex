\subsection*{Wet}
\begin{itemize}
    \item 4 T butter
    \item 2 eggs
    \item 225g greek yogurt
    \item 225g water
\end{itemize}
Get butter melting.

Beat eggs in small bowl.

Add yogurt and combine thoroughly.

Add water and combine thoroughly.


\vfill\null\columnbreak

\subsection*{Dry}
\begin{itemize}
    \item 1 \nicefrac{1}{4} c flour
    \item 2 T sugar
    \item \nicefrac{3}{4} t baking powder
    \item \nicefrac{1}{2} t baking soda
    \item \nicefrac{3}{4} t salt
\end{itemize}
Whisk together.

Pour in wet ingredients + melted butter. Stir until just combined.

Makes about 3 large waffles in our machine.
